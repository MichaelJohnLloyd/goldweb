Who We Are

Experience

Each member of this quirky quartet is a multi-talented artist, honing their skills with over 20 years of experience performing and touring at a professional level. Gold will bring their high energy to your party or function, so make sure you bring your dancing shoes!

Repertoire

Playing the most iconic sounds from the 1970s through to the 2000s, Gold is guaranteed to get you dancing, with their unique blend of dance, pop, R&B and soul. Gold deliver an authentic experience which will let you relive your party era.
